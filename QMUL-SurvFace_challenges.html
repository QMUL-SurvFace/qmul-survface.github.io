    Data Partition

    To benchmark the evaluation protocol, we firstly need to split the entire QMUL-SurvFace data into the training and test sets. Specifically, we divide the 10,638 persons each with 2 or more face images into two halves: one half (5,319) as the training data, the other half (5,319) plus the remaining 4,935 single-shot identities (in total 10,254) as the test data. These statistics are summarsied in the Table below. The same data partition is applied to both face identification and face verification challenges.
